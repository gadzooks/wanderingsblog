    HIKING TO TENGBOCHE: NEPAL'S EVEREST BASE.

    My recent hike to Everest Base Camp in Tengboche, Nepal was the adventure of a lifetime. The scenery was absolutely breathtaking as I hiked through the Sherpa villages and the glacial valleys, passing monasteries and loved soaking in the culture. I felt so small compared to the magnitude of Mt. Everest in the background, but also so grateful to be able to experience such a majestic destination. Regardless of how challenging the hike was, it was without a doubt one of the most rewarding experiences I’ve ever had.
