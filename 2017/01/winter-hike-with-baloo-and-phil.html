    WINTER HIKE WITH BALOO AND PHIL

    My weekend winter hike to Lake Serene in 2017 was one I will never forget! My friend Phil and our pup Baloo joined me for the hike, and we enjoyed the breathtaking views of the snow-blanketed mountains. To add a bit of a challenge, Phil suggested an extended trail- but we had no issues with the quaint, evergreen-laden path! We eventually made our way to Lake Serene, and the beauty of the lake surrounded by mountains was the perfect way to end our adventure.
