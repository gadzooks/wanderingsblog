    KENAI FJORDS NATIONAL PARK ALASKA

    You know you are in for a treat when you go whale watching in the Kenai Fjords national parks in Alaska. This was my second time in Seward and I went with the trusted Major Marines Tours !. I think we picked the 6 or 7 hour version (the longer the better I feel)

    Some of the highlights of the trip were :

    • Seeing Humpback whales breaching and playing with each other
    • Seeing massive glaciers up close
    • Puffins, otters, bald eagles
    • Amazing drinks they make off the freshly harvested ice from the glacier
